    NIC's contribution to the Digital India Program

    • e-Office

      e-Office paves way for an efficient and Open Government with a suite of products to go paperless and streamline workflows for both intra- and inter-government processes. The product is built on Open Architecture.

      • 271+ Departments

      • 187119+ Users

      • 7,29,613+ eFiles

      • 1.567 Cr+ eReceipts

    • e-Hospital

      eHospital@NIC is a one-stop solution for addressing these concerns and connecting patients, hospitals and doctors on the digital platform. It's a Hospital Management Information System for managing key functional areas and processes of hospitals.

      • 201 Hospitals

      • 12.84 Lakh+ Appointments

      • 2.59 Cr+ OPD Registrations

      • 12.87 Lakh ORS Appointments

    • MyGov

      MyGov is a unique citizen engagement and crowd sourcing platform for policy formulation and programme implementation. The platform forges citizen and government partnership while leveraging technology to drive the inclusive growth.

      • 53.67 Lakh+ Registered Users

      • 725+ Tasks

      • 765+ Discussions

      • 63 Groups

    • National Scholarships Portal

      National Scholarship Portal is a one stop end-to-end digital, paperless solution to streamline the scholarship application process and disbursement of funds directly to bank accounts.

      • 52+ Schemes

      • 1.2 Cr Fresh Registrations

      • 46.9 Lakh Renewal Applications

      • 1,607 Cr + Disbursement

    Shri Ram Bilas Paswan Minister for Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution

    Use of point of sale (PoS) machines in ration shops and use of Aadhaar has helped in reducing leakages under PDS. Eighteen crore ration cards have been linked to Aadhaar cards in the country. This has resulted in savings of Rs 14,000 crore due to weeding out of bogus ration cards.
